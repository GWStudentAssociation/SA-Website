Mission



The mission of The George Washington University Student Association is to further the interests and promote the welfare of all students at GW. Our responsibilities are defined in four ways:

Advocate

Whether it is lobbying for more accessible mental health resources, internship credit, or a fall break, we are first and foremost committed to representing the entire undergraduate and graduate population's interests.

Allocate

The SA Senate Finance Committee is responsible for distributing $1,200,000 to student organizations on campus.

Advertise

With every GW student being a member of the Student Association, our network reaches all of GW's 25,000 students. We are positioned to support student organizations by advertising and promoting programing and efforts.
