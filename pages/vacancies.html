Vacant Senate Positions



Being a Student Association Senator is a unique opportunity at GW for students to advocate on behalf of the student body, help in the allocation of over $1,200,000 to student organizations on campus, assist students who need help, and advertise University-wide events.

These positions are some of the most rewarding ways to get involved at the George Washington University. All members of the Student Association gain valuable communication skills, critical-thinking abilities, and leadership experience. We highly encourage you to apply to be a student leader on campus!

Freshman Positions

The Student Association is seeking hardworking and driven freshmen to act as leaders on campus. All freshmen are encouraged to apply for positions in the SA as a Freshman Senator, Freshman Advisory Council Representative, or Senate Committee Aide. These opportunities allow student leaders to work directly with GW administrators, implement important university policies, and contribute to campus events and happenings. These unpaid positions are highly competitive and the application process will be handled with the utmost care. All applications will be reviewed, and except in the event of extenuating circumstances, all applicants will be interviewed.

Further position descriptions and the official application can be found here. Applications are due Friday, September 30th at 2:00pm to the Chairperson Pro Tempore of the Senate Evan Bursey.
