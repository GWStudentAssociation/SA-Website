Vacant Senate Positions


 

Being a Student Association Senator is a unique opportunity at GW for students to advocate on behalf of the student body, help in the allocation of over $1,200,000 to student organizations on campus, assist students who need help, and advertise University-wide events.

These positions are some of the most rewarding ways to get involved at the George Washington University. All members of the Student Association gain valuable communication skills, critical-thinking abilities, and leadership experience. We highly encourage you to apply to be a student leader on campus!
