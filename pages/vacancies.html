Vacant Senate Positions



Being a Student Association Senator is a unique opportunity at GW for students to advocate on behalf of the student body, help in the allocation of over $1,200,000 to student organizations on campus, assist students who need help, and advertise University-wide events.

The Senate currently has the following vacancies:

  • (1) CCAS-U
  • (1) ESIA-U
  • (1) GWSB-G

If you are interested in joining the SA, applications are available here. Applications are due Friday, April 1st at 5:00pm to the Chairperson Pro Tempore of the Senate Evan Bursey.
