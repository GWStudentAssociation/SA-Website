Contact Us


The Student Association is committed to being an advocacy organization for the GW student body. As representatives, we hope to maintain accessibility with all GW students, student organizations and affiliates. Please feel free to reach out at any time and we will be happy to assist you. Our office is located in Marvin Center 427, the Student Advocacy Center.

Feel free to email us, contact us via Facebook or Twitter, use the form below to get in touch, or tell us about your great ideas on GW-Voice.
