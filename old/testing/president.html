President



Erika Feinman is the Student Association President. They are a senior in the Columbian College of Arts and Sciences double-majoring in Political Science and Women's Studies. As President, they are directly supported by a group of students known as the Executive Cabinet. The Executive Cabinet is made up of representatives dedicated to every issue on campus, from Academic Affairs to Diversity and Inclusion. The Vice Presidents and Directors can be emailed with questions or input regarding their specific topic areas.

Recent accomplishments from the Executive include moving Mental Health Services to the basement level of the Marvin Center so that students have better access to these services. As well, the Executive was responsible for putting student resources on the back of GWorld cards so that even in a crisis situation, students can feel safe.

This year, Erika and their team are dedicated to improving academic opportunities, increasing affordability, and amplifying student voices.

To contact Erika, email them at erika10@gwmail.gwu.edu. Office Hours are by appointment - if you have a question or concern please reach out.
