Judicial Branch

The Student Court has the jurisdiction to adjudicate disputes and determine constitutionality of issues between parties within the SA, the Joint Elections Committee, any and all funding disputes among registered student organizations, and any cases or controversies not specifically outlined in the SA Constitution.
