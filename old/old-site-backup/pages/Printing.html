Printing

The SA offers a free printing and copying service to student orgs wanting to make pamphlets, flyers, and posters for general use around campus. Student Orgs only need to bring the paper. If your student org is interested in printing or copying please email the Office Manager for Marvin 427 here. Please allow two days in advance for printing requests.
