The Senate

 Thomas Falcigno is the Executive Vice President (EVP) of  the Student Association. Thomas is a Junior in the  Columbian College of Arts and Science and is actively  involved in Greek Life. Thomas oversees the Senate, a  group  of elected undergraduate and graduate students  from each school who make up the legislative body of the  SA. 

 The Senate is divided into three committees. The Finance  Committee which is responsible for allocating and  managing $1,200,000 in student fee money that is c  collected each year. They allocate the money to over 400  student  organizations that are registered on campus. The  money supports events and operational expenses for the different  Student Organizations. 

 The Senate Student Life Committee is tasked with advocating for improvements to student life on campus. This  includes all on-campus issues outside of the classroom -- in the past, the Student Life Committee has worked on  projects ranging to improvements to dining to changes to 4-Ride. Sean Kumnick is the chairman for this committee and  can be contacted here.

The Academic Affairs Committee is the main academic advocacy group in the SA Senate. They focus their attention on issues surrounding advising, libraries, and all of the other aspects of academic life on campus. Alyssa Weekly is the chairwoman for this committee and can be contacted here.
